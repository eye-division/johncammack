Educational courses

John can design and deliver financial management modules to fit into courses offered by educational insitutions in, for example, development studies. The content may be a mixture of existing or new material. The course can be developed to achieve current learning outcomes.

A module can be concentrated over a number of days or be delivered at individual sessions over a longer period. Materials can also be developed for e-learning and distance learning courses.

John has wide experience of teaching at university level on masters and diploma courses.

Contact John to discuss your own organisation's needs.
