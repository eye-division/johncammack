John Cammack is a highly experienced advisor, consultant and trainer for the international not-for-profit/NGO sector. He was previously head of international finance at Oxfam GB and senior lecturer in accounting and financial management at Oxford Brookes University.

John is a qualified accountant, manager and teacher, who holds an MSc in international development management and an MBA. He has worked with a wide range of agencies in over 50 countries, working with groups at all levels. His publications are widely used in the NGO sector. John was named as Global Business Insight Awards NGO Consulting firm of the year 2019.
