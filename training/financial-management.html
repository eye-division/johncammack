Courses are available for a range of financial topics, drawing on John's wide experience in the NGO sector. The courses are participatory, designed to meet the needs of the individual group and to provide practical tools to be used immediately. All courses are supported with clear materials for participants to take away.

Courses include:

  • Building financial management capacity with partners
  • Financial management for the non-specialist
  • Budgeting
  • Financial management for governing bodies/trustees
  • Communicating financial management effectively
