John Cammack offers specialised training which combines NGO financial management with organisational management and international development. He delivers participatory and practical workshops with:

  • NGOs, in-house, and their partner organisations, with follow-up support
  • 'umbrella' groups providing training for their member organisations
  • university and college courses

John also offers courses through Bond, IMA International, Intrac and InterChange People Development.
