Do you find it difficult to understand what finance people mean? Do people 'switch off' when you talk about finance?

Many NGOs claim that communication between their finance staff and non-finance staff is not effective because:

  • finance messages are not fully understood by non-finance teams and partners
  • training is not provided to help finance teams to be better communicators
  • finance is not used to help improve programme impact
  • information is prepared to suit accountants' needs only

John specialises in group training, one-to-one coaching and consultancy for those who need to improve their dialogue between non-finance and finance staff, and with partners, donors and other stakeholders in a jargon free and non-technical way.
